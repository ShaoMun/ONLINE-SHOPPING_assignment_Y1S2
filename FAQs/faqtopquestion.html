Top Questions

Q: What are your delivery options and how much is shipping?
There are 2 options for delivery: Standard & Express. Standard delivery cost RM30 and Express delivery cost RM60.
Q: How can I use my points?
You can use your points to redeem products from our store.
Q: What is the return and exchange policy?
All returns and exchanges due to change of mind are subject to a 30-day return and exchange policy upon receipt of order while item is in original purchase condition.
Q: What will happen to my expired points?
Earned points that has expired will not be refunded.
Q: How does the Points Expiry Policy work?
Points earned will expire 1 YEAR at the end of the equivalent month in which they were earned. (E.g. Points earned in November 2023 will expire in 30th November 2024)
